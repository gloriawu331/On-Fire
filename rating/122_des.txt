Fragrance Family: Warm & SpicyScent Type: Cool SpicesKey Notes: Cardamom, Nutmeg, LeatherAbout: Egypt was born from parfumeur Albert Fouquet’s passion for Egyptian culture. In addition to enjoying lengthy tours of the Great Pyramid of Giza, Fouquet’s visit was also an opportunity to research new extraordinary essences. Fouquet carefully infused the exotic plants and flowers he discovered into this spicy, woody fragrance for a sophisticated, subtle, yet authentic and vibrant experience.
