What it is:A five-piece set of shaving essentials for a smoother, cleaner, and closer shave.
What it is formulated to do:The Lexington Collection™ Shave Set features The Art of Shaving® Lexington Collection™ Razor, the most technologically advanced manual razor with Flexball™ technology that responds to facial contours. It pivots in multiple directions for maximum contact with the skin for a cleaner, closer shave with fewer strokes. The Pre-Shave Oil helps protect against irritation and razor burn by promoting razor glide and is suitable for all beard types. Shaving Cream softens and lifts the beard for a close and comfortable shave. The Shaving Brush generates a rich, warm lather to help soften and lift the beard hair for a cleaner, close shave. And After-Shave Balm refreshes and hydrates to soothe the skin after shaving.
This set contains:- Razor with cartridge
- Shaving brush
- 1 oz Pre-Shave Oil
- 1.5 oz Shaving Cream
- 1 oz After-Shave Balm

What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates - Phthalates
