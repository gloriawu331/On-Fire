What it is:
A dual-ended wand that pairs the feminine Glam Jasmine fragrance with an alluring lip luster in the Icon shade.

What it does
 This innovative, double-ended tube contains lip luster gloss in one side and the fragrance in the other. It fits neatly in a purse or makeup bag and is ideal for travel.

Glam Jasmine is an alluring and overtly feminine fragrance featuring a lush bouquet of jasmine tempered by creamy sandalwood and sweetened with cassis. The effect is a divine and stunning tribute to the glamorous woman who takes center stage.

Notes:
Cassis, Jasmine, Sandalwood.
 Style:
Feminine. Floral. Head-turning.
