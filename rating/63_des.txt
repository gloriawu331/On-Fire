What it is:
 An antiaging, softening, and plumping lip serum with retinol and essential vitamins for a younger, healthy-looking pucker. 
If you want to know more…
 The Lip Serum is packed with retinol 8, a stabilized vitamin A complex that’s eight times more effective than standard retinol. It works to increase the skin´s natural production of collagen and reduce the appearance of minor dark spots, fine lines, and wrinkles around the lips. The complex contains hyaluronic acid and peptides to boost hydration with an irresistible plumping effect. Lips look and feel hydrated, even-toned, soft, supple, younger, and primed for lip color as the serum works to prevent lipstick from bleeding.
What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
