What it is:A high-performance daytime emulsion that taps into the power of skin’s natural daytime defense gene to boost resistance against environmental damage and fundamentally improve skin quality from within.

What it is formulated to do:This luxurious cream features anti-pollution powder to prevent pollutants from adhering to and damaging skin. Skingenecell 1P prevents the age-accelerating effects of Serpin B3 that lead to all signs of aging while signature botanical and moisturizing ingredients hydrate, plump, and smooth skin. It improves the overall quality of skin, reduces the appearance of wrinkles, sagging, and visible pores, and promotes skin radiance, clarity, and resilience.

Research results:In a vitro test on 100 women after 1 week:
- 93% softer skin
- 82% smoother skin
- 74% improved skin condition overall
- 78% improved skin resilience
- 64% improved sagging
In a vitro test on 100 women after 4 weeks:
- 99% softer skin
- 94% smother skin
- 90% improved skin condition overall
