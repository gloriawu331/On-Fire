What it is:A powerful duo of antiaging products that offers professional, clinically proven solutions to diminish visible signs of aging.

This technology is known to trigger the body’s natural response to generate new collagen fibers. This silky, soft textile is carefully infused with copper technology, which has been clinically proven to help reduce the appearance of wrinkles for smoother, younger‐looking skin in as little as four weeks.
