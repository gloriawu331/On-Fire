What it is:
A highlighting stick for a radiant glow.

What it does:
The Strobing Highlighting Stick features a cream-to-powder texture and imparts a healthy-glow effect. Perfect for gliding on after you apply concealer and foundation, it blends perfectly onto the complexion without leaving an ashy finish. Easy to apply and enriched with natural extracts of argan oil with beneficial properties, the stick leaves the complexion with a radiant finish.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
