What it is:
 A conditioning treatment toner that saturates the skin with its hydrating and purifying ingredients to smooth skin’s texture and protect against dehydration.

Solutions for:
- Dryness
- Dullness and uneven skin tone
- Fine lines and wrinkles

If you want to know more…
 Fresh Crème Ancienne Infusion is a conditioning treatment toner that saturates the skin with hydration. Its blend of nourishing bamboo extract, purifying lavender water, and soothing rose flower water hydrates while hyaluronic acid seals in moisture and promotes suppleness. The formula is further enriched with meadowfoam seed oil, a unique fatty acid that provides the ultimate nourishment, and seabuckthorn oil, which is rich in restorative omegas 3, 6, 7, and 9 and protective antioxidants.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:

This cream served as the muse for this supremely nourishing collection. "Lev Glazman, Fresh Co-founder
