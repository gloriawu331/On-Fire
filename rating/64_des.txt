What it is: A pair of nourishing treatments that deliver a smooth, barely-there lip look.

- Dryness
If you want to know more… Get a smooth, barely-there lip look with these travel-size treatments. What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates- Phthalates
