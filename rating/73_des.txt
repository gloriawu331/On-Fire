The first fragrance from Tory Burch captures classic elements in unexpected ways: feminine and tomboy, easy and polished. Floral peony and tuberose blend with crisp citrus notes of grapefruit and neroli anchored by earthy vetivera bright and complex mix. The fragrance comes in a super-chic glass bottle with a signature fretwork top.
