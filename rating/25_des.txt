What it is:
A transfer-proof, bold lipstick with an ultra-matte finish.

What it does:
Bold, elegant, and comfortable, Matte Liquid Lipcolor features an ultra-pigmented liquid formula that gives lips a matte, opaque finish in just one application. This long-lasting, rapid-dry formula guarantees that your lip color lasts and lasts. Its gel texture ensures a smooth application and a soft, creamy feel.
