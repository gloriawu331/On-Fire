What it is:
A pen-like, waterproof liquid eyeliner with incredible staying power for clear lines that last up to 24 hours.

What it is formulated to do:
This liquid black eyeliner creates instant, clear lines to define eyes in lasting black pigment. The thickness of the lines can be varied by simply tilting the pen, making everyday drama or high eye art a snap. Once dry, the formula doesn’t budge or smudge, so your look stays put for up to 24 hours.What it is:
