What it is:A four-piece travel collection of iconic hand creams to nourish on the go with the limited-edition pouch designed by the famous French illustrator. Charlotte Gastaut.
Solutions for:- Dryness
If you want to know more… Give your hands the gift of moisture with a scent for every mood. The Delightful Rose Hand Creams is enriched with 20 percent shea butter to lock moisture into the skin and protect it. The mouthwatering Almond Delicious Hands contains almond milk and almond oil for silky-smooth hands, the feminine Cherry Blossom Hand Cream is enriched with shea butter and cherry extract to soften with a delicate scent, and the blooming Peony like a caress of a peony petal, helps to soften the skin and nourish itthanks to shea butter. It envelops the hands with a green floral scent.
This set contains:- 0.3 oz/ 10 mL Delightful Rose Hand Cream
- 0.3 oz/ 10 mL Delicious Almond Hand Cream
- 0.3 oz/ 10 mL Cherry Blossom Hand Cream
- 0.3 oz/ 10 mL Peony Hand Cream
- Limited-edition travel pouch
What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates- Phthalates
