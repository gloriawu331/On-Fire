If you want to know more…
The softening and balancing Sensitive Cleansing Oil is infused with anti-inflammatory plant extracts to improve the texture of even the most dry and sensitive skin. It dissolves surface impurities and makeup on contact without leaving skin feeling tight and stripped. A blend of powerful, restorative almond and sweet almond oils nourish, moisturize, and protect against irritation, while andiroba oil, long used by indigenous people of the Amazon rainforest, teams with rich fatty acids (unsaturated oleic and linoleic acids) to visibly reduce inflammation and promote natural skin renewal. Skin is left feeling moisturized, nourished, and radiant.
What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates
