Kopari's Hawaiian-born co-founder, and certified organic chef, Kiana Cabell, wanted to create luxurious, coconut oil-based products for head-to-toe beauty. The company believes products crafted from the purest, premium materials perform, smell, and feel the best, while simplifying any beauty routine.
Research results:
In a consumer test study on 105 individuals, over 28 days:
- 99% said the product reduced the appearance of dry skin
- 97% said the eye area appeared smoother and more supple
- 95% reported skin looked more radiant
- 91% noticed skin elasticity was improved

If you want to know more…
 Pure and simple Coconut Sheer Oil is a unique formula that intensifies the hydration benefits of coconut oil. This lightweight, deliciously scented, and fast-absorbing treatment is great for all hair and skin types and jam packed with antiaging properties. It delivers the perfect amount of day and night moisture, plus fatty acids and rich antioxidants to visibly protect and repair the hair and skin. The non-greasy blend is infused with shea tris to visibly slow collagen breakdown, reduce UV exposure damage, and perk up elasticity in skinrestoring a supple, youthful appearance. 
What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates
What else you need to know:
 This product is hypoallergenic, gluten-free, cruelty-free, silicone-free, and non-GMO.
