What it is:A unique two-in-one serum, featuring moisturizing and antiaging benefits in a luxurious formula.
What it is formulated to do: Super Aqua-Serum BB Hydra+ deeply hydrates, re-plumps, and gives the skin more resistance to visible signs of aging.  It neutralizes free radicals and supports the natural production of collagen, glyco-aminoglycane, elastin, and fibulin.     
     
What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates - Phthalates
