What it is:
 A unique mascara that thickens, lengthens, and curls lashes without creating a clumpy look.

What it does:
Natasha's innovative mascara makes lashes stand out for those who love the look of thick, long lashes. It does not run, smudge, and has zero fallout.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:
"My newest addition to my Blackest Black Collection. I wanted to create a blackest black mascara that is buildable. Depending on how many coats, you can take your lashes from natural looking to ultra-long and thick without looking clumpy. The brush is innovative and has never been seen before. Its shape helps to separate and curl lashes without using a lash curler."Natasha Denona
