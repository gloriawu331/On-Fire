What it is:A heat-activated thickening mist scientifically designed to expand the look of hair, add shine, and protect from free radicals for a glamorous finish and color that won’t fade.
What it is formulated to do:Divine Thickening Mist is a rich mineral-infused polymer formula based on synergistic action to deliver sun protection against UVA/UVB rays and provide hair with body and manageability. Conditioning agents such as ammonyx CETAC create a smooth feel and high shine while lychee extract, an excellent free radical scavenger, slows color fading.What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates- PhthalatesWhat else you need to know:While blow drying, spray the Divine Thickening Mist and massage into hair to give body.What it is:A heat-activated thickening mist scientifically designed to expand the look of hair, add shine, and protect from free radicals for a glamorous finish and color that won’t fade.
