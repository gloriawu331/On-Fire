What it is:
 A soft, creamy, medium-to-full coverage concealer that covers imperfections while creating an

invisible finish.


What it does:
The perfect tool to brighten shadowy areas and freshen up the complexion all day long, Radiance Concealer features an ultra-light liquid texture for easy touch-ups. Now infused with peptides, it helps to strengthen the eye contour area and reduce puffiness. It lightens up darker skin tones without turning ashy, thanks to it special-designed shimmer free formula that leaves a radiant complexion with a natural-looking, matte finish.


What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens

- Sulfates
