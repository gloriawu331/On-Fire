What it is:
 A luxurious no-foam cleansing conditioner that cleanses the hair and scalp while nourishing and conditioning curls.

What it is formulated to do:
 Curl Immersion™ Coconut Cream Cleansing Conditioner is specially formulated to cleanse and nourish delicate, kinky curls. The rich, no-foam formula is enhanced with Abyssinian oil to add shine, improve manageability, and strengthen hair fibers, while mafura butter softens the hair cuticle to add deep moisturization. It gently cleanses dulling build-up in curls without drying or stripping the hair’s natural oils.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
