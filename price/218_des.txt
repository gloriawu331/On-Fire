What it is:
 A sulfate-free cleanser that builds thickness and volume, protects color, and improves the overall appearance of fine, thin hair.

What it is formulated to do:
This lightweight treatment styler adds to the diameter of each strand to thicken and strengthen hair. Marine Plumping Complex, which contains omega-3 and Seasilk®, also strengthens and restores hair’s natural thickness to reduce breakage. Color Hold®, a proprietary technology, locks in hair color brightness.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:
 This product is also free of synthetic color, DEA/TEA,  and petrochemicals, and is suited for color-treated hair. It is part of the first luxury hair care collection inspired by skin-plumping technology for supremely voluminous hair.What it is:
