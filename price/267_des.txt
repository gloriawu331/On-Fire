What it is:
 A mini duo with a high-tech, high-shine gloss and a translucent mattifying powderboth formulated with brightening pigments for a radiant, camera-ready complexion.

What it does:
 The Flash Photo Kit gives you two beauty essentials for the perfect picture. Flash Photo Gloss is a high-tech, high-shine gloss designed to enhance your perfect pout. It contains cool blue pearls that bring out the bright in your smile so you can stand out in photos and in person. It’s infused with an addictive vanilla scent, and it’s perfect for wearing alone or popping on top of your favorite lipstick. 

Flash Photo Powder is a translucent, sheer finishing powder with a blue hue that instantly brightens for universally flattering, camera-ready skin. It contains light-reflecting blue pigments for a luminous complexion. This ultra-soft, lightweight powder wears comfortably throughout the day. It also absorbs oilperfect for touch-ups on the go. 

This set contains:
- 0.1 oz/ 3 mL Flash Photo Gloss
- 0.07 oz/ 2.07 mL Flash Photo Powder
