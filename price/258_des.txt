What it is:A light textured and non-greasy antiaging cream with energizing and nourishing maple extract.
What it is formulated to do:This oil-free moisturizing and antiaging cream was especially developed to hydrate, firm, and smooth men’s skin and can be used all over the face, including the eye area. Its light texture absorbs quickly making it ideal for everyday use, even on oily or combination skin. Rich in minerals, maple extract is appreciated for its highly hydrating, nourishing, and energizing propertiesleaving skin soft, moisturized, and revitalized. Walnut extract improves the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, natural polysaccharides increase elasticity and help tighten the look of skin, and natural betaine reduces signs of fatigue around the eyes and treats dark circles.
