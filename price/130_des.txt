What it is:
A micro-fine loose powder formulated with patented Japanese ingredients that are blended together to leave skin with a thin, lightweight veil of powder with a silky finish.
 
What it does:
 The Gossamer Loose Powder compact features a first-to-market, unique silicone sifter that provides an ultrasoft texture that creates sheer coverage and brightens and evens the complexion.
