What it is:A set of three portable sunscreen brushes for easy application, a natural tint of color, and SPF 50 protection from harmful UVA/UVB rays. 
What it is formulated to do:Sunforgettable® delivers both protection and perfectionensuring the beauty of your skin today and tomorrow. Each self-dispensing, water-resistant powder sunscreen comes in a portable brush that makes sun protection application simple throughout the day. The highly refined mineral formula offers broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection with a hint of natural-looking color to keep your complexion fresh. Chemical-free and non-greasy, it’s easy to apply over makeup for shine-free touch-ups while on the go to keep you protected all day long.What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Phthalates
What else you need to know: This InStyle Award winning brush contains specialty ingredients such as zinc oxide and titanium dioxide for all natural sun protection, iron oxides for sheer, natural-looking color, and red algae which guards against environmental stresses.A portion of proceeds from every Colorescience Sunforgettable Brush go to Sunstoppable to help fight against skin cancer. Sunstoppable educates today’s children on why UV protection matters and works to “sunproof” elementary schools around the country by donating shade structures or trees.
