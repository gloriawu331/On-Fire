What it is:A box of 30 oil-free, pH balanced, and biodegradable pre-moistened wipes that gently remove face and eye makeup.   
Solutions for:- Dryness

What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates- Phthalates
What else you need to know:Dr. Lancer designed the Lancer Makeup Removing Wipes with his clients’ busy schedules in mind, and to simplify their on-the-go skin care regimen. Dr. Lancer developed these portable wipes as a superior cleansing alternative for use before Lancer The Method: Polish. The pH balanced wipes complement the Interlocking Chemistry of The Method, wherein each step, the potency and efficacy of others becomes enhanced.
