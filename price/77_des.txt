What it is:
A highlighting and sculpting palette with three powder-based, highlighter shades and three sculpting shades that last.

What it does:
The Viseart Highlight & Contour Palette features highlighters in a crystal, rose-gold, and amber hues that deliver a soft reflective finish. The sculpting shades in fair, medium, and deep deliver a sheer matte finish for natural shading. These on-trend shades flatter a number of skin tones and offer unique colors that are directly from the fashion houses of Paris.

This palette contains:
- 3 x 0.14 oz/ 3.96 g Powder-based highlighter shade
- 3 x 0.14 oz/ 3.96 g Powder-based sculpting shade

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates
