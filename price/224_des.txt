What it is:
An essential face primer with SPF 50. 

What it does:
 TOM FORD Face Protect SPF 50 delivers healthy-looking summer skin year-round. Formulated with SPF 50, this primer helps defend against UV light. It’s fortified with powerful antioxidants and soothing anti-irritants, and its lightweight and fast-absorbing formula primes and calms the look of skin, smoothing it for effortless makeup application.
