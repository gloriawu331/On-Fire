What it is:
An easily portable, liquid BB cream enriched with ginseng for a flawless-looking complexion and immediate hydration.

Solutions for:
- Uneven skin tone
- Dryness
- Dullness and uneven texture

If you want to know more…
 Held in a soft cushion, Liquid BB Crème helps to enhance the complexion and immediately moisturize your skin. Enriched with ginseng, known in Korea for its smoothing properties, the formula provides sheer coverage and helps to even out skin tone, conceal imperfections, reduce the visible signs of aging, and provide a feeling of freshness with instant hydration. Layer as you wish for more buildable coverage throughout the day.
