What it is:
 An intensive sheet mask that works to correct pigmentation, boost radiance, and deeply hydrate for a brighter, even toned complexion.

Solutions for:
- Uneven skin tone
- Dryness
- Dullness and uneven texture

If you want to know more…
 Crafted from 100 percent natural cotton, this intensive sheet mask works to correct pigmentation, boost luminosity, and deeply hydrate. Thanks to Dermapep™, it’s clinically proven to improve pigmentation and unify the skin tone more effectively than any other whitening ingredient. Niacinamide actively brightens and regenerates skin, improving tone and encouraging collagen production. Sodium hyaluronate delivers instant moisture, while allantoin and licorice root extract, work together to reduce inflammation in the skin, supporting the cellular structure to help increase moisture retention. For an at-home spa experience, just cool it in the fridge prior to use and apply directly to your face.
