If you want to know more… This multifunctional BB cream is a convergence between skin care and makeup. The cream-to-powder formula has air-fitting technology that allows the skin to breathe all day long. Enriched with 70 percent moisturizing ingredients such as oxygen water, marine complex, and phyto complex, it’ll keep skin looking as healthy and vibrant as ever by delivering anti-redness, detoxifying, and soothing benefits to the skin. Powerful broad-spectrum SPF 30 will defend against sun exposure while its anti-pollution properties will serve as a barrier against harmful dust in the environment. The moist, textured balm conceals blemishes, enlarged pores, and uneven skin tone and texture to perfect skin’s complexionleaving a naturally flawless finish.
