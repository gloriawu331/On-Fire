What it is:A set of essentials to thoroughly transform rough, dry heels and toes in just minutes a day for soft, supple, beautiful feet.

What it is formulated to do:This five-piece set works together to create soft, supple, ultra-hydrated feet. The Smart Pedi Buffing Brush Head effectively exfoliates and buffs dry, damaged feet while the Smart Pedi Disc is designed to smooth and soften dull, rough skin. Pedi-Buff contains an exfoliating blend of lactic acid, apricot seed, and a tri-fruit complex to dissolve dead skin and refine skin texture. Pedi-Balm contains a gentle blend of shea butter, honey, and apricot oil to soften, hydrate, and protect feet. Pedi-Boost exfoliates and renews skin without harsh particles. This powerful foot peel combines lactic and glycolic acid to slough and remove dead skin cells.
