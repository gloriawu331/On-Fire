What it is:
An advanced Hydrating Serum specifically designed to moisturize and support skin’s natural elasticity with all the ingredients you need to plump and hydrate your skin, nothing less, nothing more.

Solutions for:
- Dryness
- Loss of firmness and elasticity
- Fine lines and wrinkles

If you want to know more…
 The Ultimate Hydrating Serum is infused with Dr. Roebuck’s super active base, the CNYouth Molecule™, to combat dry skin. This base alone contains five actives, including hyaluronic acid and vitamin C, to help support and boost your skin’s moisture. Additionally, ubiquinone helps to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, vitamin B3 prevents the loss of moisture, and aloe vera soothes and moisturizes the skin. The thick and luxurious texture absorbs quickly into your skin for immediate hydration.

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:
 Dr Roebuck’s products are formulated with minimal ingredients for maximum results. The ingredients are hand-picked to ensure that they are the most natural and effective. It is cruelty-free, BPA-free, and free of fillers, PEGs, TEA/DEA, petrochemicals, perfume, color, and glycols.
